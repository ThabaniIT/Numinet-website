About NumiNet

Company Overview

Nash Unlimited Mining Network (NUMINET) was established in 1993 as a specialized provider of industrial tools, mechanical equipment, and repair services for the mining and industrial sectors in South Africa.

Following its integration into the Bethel Trucking Group in 2018, NUMINET expanded its operations into a diversified industrial services group, offering air conditioning and refrigeration solutions, electrical and mechanical repairs, fleet and truck services, hydraulics, fueling solutions, and industrial equipment support.

In line with regional growth, NUMINET has established a support and technical services branch in Eswatini, focused on office support, household and commercial equipment maintenance, and air conditioning installation and servicing. This expansion enables the Group to deliver reliable, high-quality technical services closer to its clients while maintaining its core industrial operations in South Africa.

Backed by certified technicians, experienced engineers, and strategic partnerships with leading equipment manufacturers, NUMINET continues to uphold its reputation for reliability, safety, and technical excellence across Southern Africa.
